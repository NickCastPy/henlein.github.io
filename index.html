strap

The strap secures the watch to your wrist. Henlein watches' straps can range from golden to ostrich, crocodile, alligator and more.

hands

The hands move over the dial itself, pointing at the hour markers to indicate the time. They are mostly made of the main material of the watch.

case

Case is the housing of the watch. Our brand exclusively uses gold, silver, platinum and rhodium to assemble cases.

crystal

The clear portion of the watch face that protects the dial, hands, etc. The crystals we produce are made of glass or synthetic saphire.
